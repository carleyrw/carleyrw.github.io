App Design

This project is a fictional app for a tour company I created called TravelJapan. This app would be used by people who are planning to go to Japan. There are other apps that allow you to plan and book your trip, and there are apps that allow you to share your experience during or after your trip. However there aren't many that allow you to easily do both in one place. This app allows you to bookmark places and sights you would like to see in Japan as well as book hotels and restaurants. The app has a menu to make "lists" of places you want to see. While you are on your vacation you can take photos and make posts. It has links to share with other popular social media websites, but is also a social media outlet itself.
